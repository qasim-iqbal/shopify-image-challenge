HOW TO CONDUCT IMAGE SEARCH?

GallerAI is an image search gallery. To search for an image, just type comma seperated keywords in search bar (e.g. shoes, shirt) or drag and drop an image either from your computer or one of the image from gallery. Currently, The image gallery can classify upto 5 different items in images including [T-Shirt, Longsleeve, Shoes, Pants, Hat]
